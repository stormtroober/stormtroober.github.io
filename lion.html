Lion

The lion (Panthera leo) is a species in the family Felidae; it is a muscular, deep-chested cat with a short, rounded head, a reduced neck, and round ears, and a hairy tuft at the end of its tail.

African Lion

The African lion (Panthera leo leo) is native to Africa and is one of the largest carnivores in Africa, second only to the polar bear in size. African lions inhabit grassy plains, savannas, and open woodlands.

Asiatic Lion

The Asiatic lion (Panthera leo persica) is a lion subspecies that exists as a single population in India's Gujarat State. Asiatic lions are slightly smaller than African lions and have a distinctive fold of skin running along their bellies.
